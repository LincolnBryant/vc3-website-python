Virtual Clusters for Community Computation

A platform for creating a single logical environment out of heterogeneous computing resources

For User Communities

Combine your resource allocations and cloud credits into virtual clusters, controlled by middleware frameworks and policies you define, shared with collaborators you choose.

High Performance and High-Throughput Computing Resource Providers

VC3 will offer new approaches to present your institutional HPC resources to scientific collaborations and individual investigators. You will be able to build hybrid systems to connect on-premise resources with those from partner institutions and commercial providers.

For Workload System and Science Gateway Developers

Deal with resources in the abstract when you want to, or get more details when you need them. The VC3 platform will host modular services to support your workload management system or science gateway.
